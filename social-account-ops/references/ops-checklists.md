# Ops Checklists

## Intake Questions

- What account is being operated, and on which platform?
- Who is the exact audience, and what urgent problem do they have?
- What offer, product, or personal brand should the content support?
- What proof exists already: results, screenshots, stories, demos, testimonials?
- What is the real goal for the next 30 days: reach, followers, leads, or sales?
- What constraints exist: legal, tone, brand, review flow, production capacity?

## Weekly Operating Loop

1. Review last week's winners and losers.
2. Extract reusable hooks, angles, and objections.
3. Plan the next batch around a small number of content pillars.
4. Produce platform-native drafts.
5. Publish on a realistic cadence.
6. Reply to comments and collect language from the audience.
7. Record learnings and define the next experiments.
