# Platform Playbook

## Xiaohongshu

- Optimize for search, saves, and trust.
- Prefer useful, specific, experience-based notes over abstract slogans.
- Use formats such as problem/solution, checklist, comparison, diary, and before/after.
- Structure each post as promise, context, 3 to 5 concrete points, proof, and CTA.
- Track saves, shares, profile visits, and inbound messages.

## X

- Optimize for attention, opinion, and network effects.
- Prefer clear takes, fast reactions, founder voice, and compact threads.
- Use formats such as sharp one-liners, mini-essays, threads, and build-in-public updates.
- Structure each post as hook, point of view, supporting detail, and conversation CTA.
- Track impressions, profile clicks, replies, reposts, and follows.

## Douyin

- Optimize for stop-scroll, watch time, and action.
- Prefer visual proof, strong first 2 seconds, and one clean idea per video.
- Use formats such as hook-demo-proof-CTA, talking head, screen demo, testimonial, and transformation.
- Structure each video as opening pattern break, promise, proof, payoff, and CTA.
- Track 2-second hold, completion rate, shares, follows, and conversion actions.

## Cross-Platform Rules

- Do not publish the same asset unchanged across all platforms.
- Match tone and pacing to the platform's native behavior.
- Reuse the same core idea only after adapting hook, structure, and CTA.
- Favor proof and specificity over brand adjectives.
