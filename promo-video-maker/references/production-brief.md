# Production Brief

## Required Fields

- campaign or product name
- audience
- placement
- duration
- CTA
- mandatory claims and legal constraints
- available assets

## Shot List Fields

- timecode
- shot description
- on-screen text
- voiceover
- visual proof
- transition or motion note
- asset source

## Editor Handoff Checklist

- final script or beat sheet
- aspect ratios to export
- caption copy
- music direction
- graphic or subtitle notes
- versioning plan by placement
- review criteria
