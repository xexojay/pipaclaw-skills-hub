# Video Formats

## 15-Second Short Ad

- Goal: earn attention and drive one clear action.
- Structure: pattern break, promise, proof, CTA.
- Best for paid social, short feed placements, and remarketing.

## 30-Second Product Promo

- Goal: explain value fast without losing pace.
- Structure: hook, problem, solution, proof, CTA.
- Best for launches, landing page embeds, and creator-style promos.

## 60 to 90-Second Explainer

- Goal: build belief through context and demo.
- Structure: problem, stakes, reframing, demo, proof, CTA.
- Best for homepage hero videos, sales support, and event intros.

## Event Teaser

- Goal: create anticipation and urgency.
- Structure: reveal, social proof, countdown, CTA.
- Best for conferences, webinars, launches, and community events.

## App Demo

- Goal: show workflow value with minimal narration.
- Structure: job to be done, key screens, payoff, CTA.
- Best for SaaS, mobile apps, and product launches.
