# Control Room

Use this skill like a visible production control room.

For non-trivial work, report:
- `Stage`
- `Current Owner`
- `Current Gate`
- `Current Artifact`
- `Estimated Credits`
- `Running Jobs`
- `Project`
- `Blocker`
- `Next Action`

## Role ownership

- `Showrunner`
  Owns mode detection, brief framing, production path, and escalation.

- `Head Writer`
  Owns story premise, outline, script, dialogue, and narrative revisions.

- `Character Director`
  Owns character cards, look direction, and character visual consistency.

- `Scene Director`
  Owns locations, environment logic, and scene visual continuity.

- `Storyboard Director`
  Owns beats, shots, camera intent, and scene-to-scene visual flow.

- `Voice Director`
  Owns dialogue delivery, narration strategy, and speaker mapping.

- `Music Director`
  Owns BGM strategy, cues, emotional arc, and non-dialogue audio planning.

- `Edit Director`
  Owns pacing, cut plan, transition logic, subtitles, and final assembly brief.

- `Pipeline Operator`
  Owns API execution, task polling, persistence, and workspace handoff.

## Reporting rule

Speak in terms of stage ownership:
- what stage the work is in
- which role owns the blocker
- what artifact just completed
- what happens next
